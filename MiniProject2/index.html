FAQs

Rajiv Gandhi University of Knowledge Technologies (RGUKT) is a state university in India, established by the Government of Andhra Pradesh in 2008. It aims to provide quality education in the fields of technology and science to talented rural youth.

RGUKT offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various disciplines such as engineering, sciences, and humanities. It specializes in offering integrated six-year programs leading to a Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) degree.

Admissions to RGUKT are primarily open to rural students who have completed their 10th standard (or equivalent) with high academic performance. The admission process typically involves a written examination, followed by counseling.

Yes, RGUKT offers scholarships and financial assistance to eligible students based on merit and socioeconomic background. These scholarships help students cover tuition fees, accommodation, and other expenses related to their education.
